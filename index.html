Instagram + Google Maps App

Welcome to my Instagram + Google Maps App!

Type in start/ending addresses and click Go! The path is draggable, just like Google Maps.

Instagram pictures will show up at each turn. The pictures are draggable (left click) and can be enlarged (right click). Double Click to zoom even further.

The number of pictures at each turn can be set.

 

Enjoy!

 

P.S. PLEASE, if you care about your privacy, set your Instagram privacy settings or your personal pics might show up here
